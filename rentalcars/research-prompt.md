# Research Prompt — Rental Car Agencies in Morocco
## Use this in Perplexity & ChatGPT

---

## PROMPT TO PASTE:

---

I run a performance marketing and systems agency targeting rental car agencies in Morocco. I need deep research to understand this market and craft an irresistible monthly subscription offer for them.

Research and answer ALL of the following:

---

### 1. MARKET LANDSCAPE — Rental Car Agencies in Morocco

- How many rental car agencies operate in Morocco? Break down by city (Casablanca, Marrakech, Agadir, Fes, Tanger, Rabat, Oujda, Laayoune).
- What are the biggest players (international: Hertz, Avis, Europcar, Sixt, Budget) vs local independents?
- What percentage of the market is local independent agencies vs international franchises?
- What is the average monthly revenue of a mid-size local rental car agency in Morocco?
- What are peak seasons (Ramadan, summer, Eid, tourist season)?
- What are the main customer types: tourists (foreign), Moroccans, corporate clients, airport pickups?

---

### 2. HOW THEY CURRENTLY GET CLIENTS

- Where do rental car agencies in Morocco currently advertise? (Facebook/Instagram, Google, Booking.com, rentalcars.com, word of mouth, travel agencies?)
- Do they use Meta Ads actively? If yes, what type of creatives/offers do they run?
- Do they have CRM systems or customer databases? Or do they manage everything via WhatsApp and phone?
- How do they handle reservations? (WhatsApp, phone calls, booking forms, third-party platforms?)
- Do they follow up with past customers for repeat rentals?
- What is their average customer acquisition cost (CAC) and lifetime value (LTV)?

---

### 3. PAIN POINTS & PROBLEMS THEY FACE

- What are the biggest operational problems for local rental car agencies in Morocco?
- Do they struggle with: no-shows, last-minute cancellations, car availability tracking, driver management, invoicing, fleet maintenance tracking?
- Are they losing money by not capturing leads from Facebook/Instagram? Do they know how to run ads?
- Do they have a professional online presence (website, Google Business, Facebook page)?
- Are they struggling with seasonality — what do they do in slow months?
- Do they lose clients to larger platforms like rentalcars.com or Booking.com because they have no system?
- How do they handle deposits, contracts, damage claims?

---

### 4. WHAT SYSTEMS & SERVICES THEY NEED

List and explain what a full-stack solution for a rental car agency in Morocco should include:

- **Meta Ads system**: what campaigns to run (lead gen, retargeting, WhatsApp Click-to-Chat), what offers convert, what creatives work
- **CRM system**: how to track leads, reservations, follow-ups, repeat clients — what tools (GoHighLevel, Airtable, custom?)
- **Reservation & booking system**: online booking form, calendar sync, automated confirmations via WhatsApp
- **Fleet management**: car availability tracker, maintenance reminders, damage log
- **Invoicing & contracts**: digital contract signing, payment tracking, deposit management
- **Review & reputation system**: automated Google review requests after rental, managing negative reviews
- **Retargeting & loyalty**: WhatsApp follow-up sequences for past renters, seasonal offers
- **Reporting dashboard**: monthly report on leads, rentals, revenue, ad spend ROI

---

### 5. COMPETITIVE BENCHMARKS

- What do rental car agencies in Morocco currently pay for ads (if they run any)?
- What do similar agencies pay for software/CRM tools globally?
- What would a complete done-for-you service (ads + CRM + systems + support) be worth monthly to them?
- What is a realistic price range for a monthly retainer in the Moroccan market (in DH)?

---

### 6. OUTREACH & SALES INTELLIGENCE

- Where can I find rental car agencies in Morocco online? (Google Maps, Facebook, Instagram, directories?)
- What are the best ways to reach agency owners: WhatsApp cold outreach, Instagram DM, LinkedIn, in-person?
- What decision-maker titles should I target: owner, manager, director?
- What language do they prefer for business communication: Arabic (Darija), French, or English?
- What objections will they have to buying a monthly service? (price, trust, already have clients, don't believe in ads?)
- What proof/case studies would convince them? (CPL, ROAS, number of rentals generated?)

---

### 7. OFFER STRUCTURE RECOMMENDATION

Based on everything above, suggest:
- The ideal monthly subscription tiers (Starter / Growth / Pro) with what's included in each
- The right price points in MAD (Moroccan Dirham) for each tier
- What quick win I can deliver in month 1 to make them stay long-term
- What lock-in mechanisms ensure they don't cancel (dependency on systems, results, relationship)

---

**Output format:** structured sections matching the 7 above. Be specific with numbers, examples, and Morocco-specific context. Include any sources or platforms where more data can be found.
