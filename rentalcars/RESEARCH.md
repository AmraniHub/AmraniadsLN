# Rental Car Agencies — Morocco Market Research
## AmraniAds Service Intelligence Document

---

## 1. Market Reality

### Who they are
- Local agencies: 5–50 cars, owner-operated, family business
- Cities: Marrakech, Agadir, Casablanca, Fes, Tanger, Rabat
- Revenue model: 300–800 DH/day per car, seasonal peaks (July–September, December holidays)
- Dead months: November, January, February — biggest fear is empty fleet
- 13M+ tourists visited Morocco in 2025, ~35% rent a car

### How they currently operate
- Everything on WhatsApp + phone — no CRM, no pipeline, no tracking
- Fleet "managed" via mental notes, Excel, WhatsApp groups
- Booking confirmation = a WhatsApp message
- Contracts: paper or Word templates, signed manually on pickup
- No digital payment system (mostly cash or bank transfer)

### How they currently get clients
- Booking.com, Rentalcars.com, Expedia → 20–30% commission per booking
- Google Maps presence (inconsistent quality)
- WhatsApp referrals from previous clients
- A Facebook page that posts sporadically — no paid ads
- Almost none run proper Meta Ads with WhatsApp automation

## 2. Pain Points (Their Words)

| Pain | Reality |
|---|---|
| "Booking takes too much commission" | 20–30% on every booking — they hate it, have no alternative |
| "My cars are empty in winter" | No retargeting, no loyalty system, no off-season offer |
| "I tried Facebook ads — didn't work" | Ran generic traffic ads with no WhatsApp integration, wasted money |
| "Marketers promise everything, deliver nothing" | Burned by generic agencies → deeply skeptical of monthly retainers |
| "I don't have time to manage a CRM" | True — needs to be automatic, not manual input |
| "I don't have a website" | Most don't — and don't think they need one |

### Key insight
They will pay a monthly retainer **only if you prove ROI in extra bookings** — not "branding", not "reach", not "engagement". The conversation must always come back to: "how many direct bookings did this generate vs your Booking.com commission cost."

## 3. The Market Gap You Fill

- 60% of bookings happen online but most local agencies close them manually
- Almost no local agency runs Meta Ads with Click-to-WhatsApp
- Local CRM tools exist (Locapp, OTTA Von) but adoption is near zero
- No agency has automated WhatsApp follow-up sequences for cold leads
- Most Google Business profiles are incomplete, no review strategy

**This is a blue ocean in every Moroccan city outside the 5 big international franchises (Europcar, Hertz, Avis, Budget, Sixt).**

## 8. Outreach Strategy

### Where to find them
- Google Maps: search "location voiture [city]" → agencies with 10–50 reviews
- Facebook: search "agence location voiture [city]" — active page but no running ads
- Instagram: hashtags #locationvoiture #locationvoituremaroc #locationvoituremarrakech
- Booking.com supplier list for Morocco

### Target profile (ideal first client)
- 8–25 cars in fleet
- Has Google Business profile (even incomplete)
- Has Facebook page but no active Meta Ads
- Replies on WhatsApp (check response badge on their page)
- NOT a franchise (Europcar, Hertz, etc.)

### Cold message — WhatsApp (French)
> Bonjour, j'ai vu votre agence sur Google — vous avez de bonnes notes.
> J'aide les agences de location à générer des réservations directes via Meta Ads et WhatsApp automatisé, sans passer par Booking.com.
> Est-ce que c'est quelque chose qui vous intéresse ?

### Cold message — Darija version
> السلام عليكم، شفت وكالتكم على Google — عندكم تقييم مزيان.
> كانعاون وكالات الكراء يجيبو حجوزات مباشرة عبر Meta Ads و WhatsApp أوتوماتيك، بلا عمولة Booking.
> واش هاد الشي كيهمكم؟

### Objection handling

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "J'ai déjà essayé les pubs" | "Avec quelle agence? La différence c'est le Click-to-WhatsApp — le client vous contacte directement, pas un formulaire. On vous montre les résultats après 30 jours." |
| "C'est trop cher" | "Vous payez combien à Booking.com par mois? Si on vous génère 3 réservations directes, vous avez récupéré votre investissement + économisé la commission." |
| "Booking.com me suffit" | "Booking peut partir demain. On a vu ça avec des agences qui dépendaient à 80% d'une seule plateforme. Les réservations directes c'est votre assurance." |
| "Je n'ai pas le temps" | "Justement — le système tourne sans vous. Les leads arrivent sur votre WhatsApp, les relances partent automatiquement. Vous gérez juste les confirmations." |
| "Je n'ai pas de site web" | "Pas besoin pour commencer. Le client voit la pub, clique, et vous écrit sur WhatsApp. On peut ajouter un site plus tard si vous voulez." |

### First 5 clients strategy
- Offer 1 free month (or 50% off) to the first 3 clients
- Requirement: they let you use their results as a case study
- Goal: build 3 real before/after stories with booking numbers
- These case studies go on the landing page and in every outreach message
