# PROMPT 5 — Competitive Benchmarks: Pricing & Market Rates
## Paste this in Perplexity (web search ON) or ChatGPT

---

I'm pricing a monthly subscription service for rental car agencies in Morocco that includes Meta Ads management, CRM setup, booking systems, fleet tracking, and full operations support. I need benchmark data to price it correctly.

**What They Currently Spend on Marketing**
- What does a typical small local rental car agency in Morocco (5–20 cars) spend on advertising per month?
- Do most agencies in Morocco run paid ads at all, or is it mostly organic/word of mouth?
- If they run ads, what platforms and what monthly budget (in MAD)?
- Do they hire freelancers for social media management? What do local Moroccan freelancers charge (MAD/month)?
- Do any agencies work with marketing agencies in Morocco already? What do Moroccan agencies charge for social media + ads management?

**What They Currently Spend on Software**
- What software do rental car agencies in Morocco currently pay for? (booking systems, accounting, fleet management)
- Are most using free tools (WhatsApp, Excel, Google Sheets) with no paid software?
- What do international rental car SaaS tools cost monthly: RentWorks, Navotar, Fleetlet, HQ Rental Software?
- What does a GoHighLevel subscription cost and what does it include?
- What would a full tech stack (CRM + booking + automation + reporting) realistically cost monthly in tool fees?

**Global Benchmarks for Similar Services**
- What do marketing agencies in the US/UK/France charge rental car agencies for full-service monthly retainers (ads + CRM + systems)?
- What do car rental businesses globally spend on customer acquisition per booking?
- What is the average LTV (lifetime value) of a loyal customer for a car rental agency?
- What ROAS do rental car Meta Ads campaigns achieve on average globally?

**Morocco-Specific Pricing Context**
- What is the purchasing power reality for a small business owner in Morocco?
- What do Moroccan SMBs typically pay for monthly software subscriptions (SaaS)?
- What monthly retainer amount is considered "normal" vs "premium" for a marketing service in Morocco?
- What is the average monthly revenue of a rental agency with 10 cars — and what % of revenue would they reasonably spend on marketing + systems?
- What price point in MAD triggers "too expensive" vs "serious but fair" vs "too cheap to trust"?

**What My Service Is Worth vs What to Charge**
- Based on the above, what is the economic value I deliver if I:
  - Bring 20 qualified leads/month via Meta Ads
  - Set up a CRM that captures and converts leads
  - Automate their booking and WhatsApp follow-up
  - Save 10 hours/week of manual admin work
  - Generate 8–12 additional rentals/month
- What is that worth in MAD if average rental is 400–700 DH/day for 3 days avg = 1,200–2,100 DH per booking?
- What monthly retainer price can I justify and how do I frame the ROI?

**Competitor Audit**
- Are there any agencies in Morocco already targeting rental car agencies with digital marketing services?
- What do competitors offer and at what price?
- Is this an underserved niche in Morocco?

Output: structured by section. Include specific numbers in both USD and MAD where possible. Flag when data is estimated vs sourced.
