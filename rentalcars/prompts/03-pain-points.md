# PROMPT 3 — Pain Points & Problems of Rental Car Agencies in Morocco
## Paste this in Perplexity (web search ON) or ChatGPT

---

I want to deeply understand the daily operational and business pain points of local rental car agency owners in Morocco. I'm building a service to solve these problems. Be specific, realistic, and Morocco-context aware.

**Operational Problems**
- What are the most common daily operational headaches for a rental car agency owner in Morocco?
- How do they track which cars are available, rented, or in maintenance — Excel, whiteboard, phone calls?
- How do they manage multiple drivers and car handovers without confusion?
- How do they handle late returns, no-shows, and last-minute cancellations?
- How do they track fuel levels, mileage, and car condition before/after each rental?
- What happens when a car breaks down mid-rental — do they have a protocol?

**Financial & Administrative Problems**
- How do they handle deposits — cash, bank transfer, credit card hold?
- Do they have proper contracts for every rental, or is it done verbally/on WhatsApp?
- How do they invoice clients — handwritten receipts, Word documents, accounting software?
- Do they have visibility on their monthly revenue, profit, and expenses?
- How do they handle damage claims — is there a dispute process or do they lose money?
- Are they paying too much commission to booking platforms (Booking.com, rentalcars.com) while having no direct booking system?

**Marketing & Growth Problems**
- Do agency owners feel they are losing potential clients because they have no professional online presence?
- Are they getting inquiries on WhatsApp that they fail to convert because of slow response or no follow-up?
- Are they struggling to compete with international brands (Hertz, Avis, Europcar) that have bigger budgets and better systems?
- Do they know Meta Ads exist but don't know how to use them effectively?
- Are they stuck relying on word of mouth and seasonal tourism — with no predictable lead flow?
- Do they have no way to re-engage past clients for repeat bookings?

**Seasonal & Cash Flow Problems**
- How severely does revenue drop in slow months (Jan, Feb, Nov for non-Ramadan years)?
- What do agency owners do during slow months — reduce staff, park cars, take loans?
- Do they have any marketing strategy for slow seasons or do they just wait?

**Trust & Technology Problems**
- Are agency owners resistant to technology — prefer WhatsApp and phone over any software?
- Do they feel burned by past experiences with web developers or marketers who didn't deliver?
- Are they skeptical about paying a monthly retainer for marketing/systems they can't see direct ROI from?
- What level of digital literacy does a typical rental car agency owner in Morocco have?

**Competitive Threats**
- Are local agencies losing market share to platforms like Booking.com or rentalcars.com?
- Are larger aggregators commoditizing their service and forcing price competition?
- Is there a new generation of more tech-savvy rental competitors emerging?

**Roleplay section — answer as a Moroccan rental car agency owner in Marrakech:**
"I own 12 cars, I've been in business 8 years. What keeps me up at night about my business? What do I wish someone could solve for me? What would make me seriously consider paying 3,000–5,000 DH/month for a service?"

Output: structured by section. Be honest, raw, and specific. This is to help me build an offer that speaks directly to their real problems.
