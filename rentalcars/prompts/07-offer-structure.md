# PROMPT 7 — Offer Structure: Monthly Subscription Service for Rental Car Agencies in Morocco
## Paste this in Perplexity (web search ON) or ChatGPT

---

Based on everything known about rental car agencies in Morocco — their revenue, pain points, marketing gaps, and purchasing power — help me design the perfect monthly subscription offer. I want something irresistible, properly priced, and structured to keep clients long-term.

**Core Offer Design Principles**
- What makes a monthly retainer offer sticky for a Moroccan SMB?
- What is the difference between selling "services" vs selling "outcomes" — and which works better in Morocco?
- How do I make the offer feel like a no-brainer ROI decision rather than an expense?
- What should the guarantee or risk-reversal be? (money back? results guarantee? free month?)

**Recommended Tier Structure**
Design 3 subscription tiers for a rental car agency in Morocco. For each tier include:
- Tier name (in French or bilingual)
- Monthly price in MAD
- Exact deliverables (what's included)
- Who this tier is for (how many cars, what stage of business)
- What results/outcomes to promise

Use these constraints:
- Starter tier: accessible price, core service, builds trust
- Growth tier: main offer, 80% of clients should land here
- Pro tier: full service, flagship, positions Starter as entry-level
- Tool costs must stay under 300 USD/month for you to maintain margins
- Service must be deliverable with 1–2 people

**Month 1 Quick Win Strategy**
- What is the fastest, most visible result I can deliver in the first 30 days to make the client want to stay?
- Should I prioritize: running first ads, setting up CRM, building booking page, or getting Google reviews?
- What does a successful month 1 look like in terms of metrics I can show the client?
- How do I set expectations correctly so the client doesn't churn after month 1?

**Lock-In & Retention Mechanisms**
- What features or dependencies make it painful for the client to cancel?
  - Their entire client database lives in my system
  - Their booking page and phone number are tied to my tools
  - Their ad accounts are managed under my agency account
  - Their WhatsApp automation runs through my platform
- What relationship-building practices reduce churn for Moroccan clients?
  - Monthly review call?
  - WhatsApp group with the owner?
  - Monthly performance report PDF?
  - Quarterly strategy session?
- What is the ideal contract length: month-to-month, 3 months, 6 months, annual?

**Upsell & Expansion Revenue**
- What add-on services can I offer after month 3 to increase revenue per client?
  - Website redesign or landing page build (one-time fee)
  - WhatsApp API setup (one-time setup + monthly)
  - Google Ads management (add-on monthly)
  - Professional photo/video shoot of fleet (one-time)
  - Staff training on CRM usage (one-time)
  - Multi-location management (second agency branch)
- What is the realistic 12-month revenue per client if I upsell correctly?

**Onboarding Flow**
- What is the step-by-step onboarding process for a new rental car agency client?
  - Day 1: what accounts to get access to, what info to collect
  - Week 1: what to build first
  - Week 2–3: what to launch
  - End of month 1: what to review with the client
- What onboarding document or checklist should I give the client at the start?

**Pricing Psychology**
- Should I charge in MAD or USD with Moroccan clients?
- Should prices end in 00 (clean) or 97/99 (psychological)?
- Should I show all 3 tiers on a page or reveal pricing only on a call?
- What is the anchoring strategy: lead with Pro to make Growth look reasonable?
- Should I offer a setup/onboarding fee in addition to the monthly retainer?

**Full Offer One-Pager**
Write a complete offer one-pager in French that I can send to a rental car agency owner after a first conversation. Include:
- Headline that speaks to their pain
- What they get (3 tiers)
- What results to expect
- How it works (3-step process)
- Why us (proof/credibility)
- Call to action

Output: detailed, specific, and actionable. Include the full one-pager at the end. All prices in MAD. French language for client-facing content.
