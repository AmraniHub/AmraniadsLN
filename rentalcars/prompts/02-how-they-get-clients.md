I'm researching how rental car agencies in Morocco currently acquire customers. I need to understand their existing marketing and sales channels before I pitch them a better system. Be specific and Morocco-focused.

**Online Advertising**
- Do rental car agencies in Morocco run paid ads? Which platforms: Facebook/Instagram Meta Ads, Google Ads, TikTok Ads?
- Search Facebook Ad Library for Moroccan rental car agencies — what kind of ads are they running? (offers, creatives, CTAs, languages used: Darija, French, Arabic, English?)
- What is a typical ad budget for a small local agency in Morocco (monthly in MAD)?
- Are they running WhatsApp Click-to-Chat ads? Click-to-Messenger?
- Do they run retargeting campaigns or is it all cold traffic?
- Are they running Google Search campaigns for keywords like "location voiture Marrakech" or "car rental Agadir"?

**Booking Platforms & Aggregators**
- Which third-party platforms do Moroccan agencies list on: Booking.com, rentalcars.com, kayak, Expedia, GetYourGuide, Viator?

**Organic & Social Media**

- Do they appear on Google Business Profile (Google Maps)? Are profiles complete with photos, hours, reviews?

- Do they do any SEO or content marketing?

**Word of Mouth & Traditional**
- How much of their business comes from referrals, repeat customers, or hotel concierge partnerships?

- Do they have airport booths or partnerships with airports (Marrakech Menara, Casablanca Mohammed V, Agadir Al Massira)?

**WhatsApp & Phone**
- Is WhatsApp the primary communication channel for reservations?
- Do they have a WhatsApp Business account set up properly (catalog, auto-replies, labels)?
- How do they handle inbound leads that come via WhatsApp — manually or with any automation?
- How fast do they respond to WhatsApp inquiries on average?

**Lead Management**
- When a lead contacts them, what happens? Do they have a sales process or is it ad hoc?
- Do they follow up with leads who didn't convert?
- Do they have any system to capture and store customer contact info?
- Do they send any follow-up messages to past customers for repeat business?

Output: structured by each section. Be specific. Include examples of real agency Facebook pages or Google listings if you find them.
