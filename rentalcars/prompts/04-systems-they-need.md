  - What offers convert best: discount %, free GPS, free delivery to airport, no-deposit weekend deals?
  - What creatives perform: video walkaround of car, before/after client review, price comparison?
  - What languages: Darija, French, English, Spanish (for Tanger/Tetouan)?
  - What does a full funnel look like: cold → warm → WhatsApp → booked?
- What is a realistic monthly ad budget for a small agency to generate meaningful results?
- What KPIs should I track and report: CPL, cost per reservation, ROAS?

**2. CRM & Lead Management System**
- What CRM features does a rental car agency need?

  - Automatic WhatsApp notification when a new lead comes in

  - Re-engagement sequences for past clients
- What tools can deliver this for a Moroccan agency: GoHighLevel, HubSpot Free, Airtable, Notion, custom build?
- What is the simplest setup that actually gets used by a non-tech agency owner?

**3. Booking & Reservation System**
- What does a proper online booking flow look like for a rental car agency?
  - Online form: car selection, pickup/return dates, location, contact info

- How to monitor and respond to reviews (Google, Facebook, Booking.com)?
